Scott Small

Scott is a senior developer and team lead at Semaphore Solutions, a genomics labratory services provider based out of Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. He has over twenty years experience as a systems developer, having previously worked in the digital publishing, higher education, performance marketing, and travel industries.

When he's not hacking away at work, or on a side project, he fuels a passion for travel. Having experienced four continents, and over twenty-six countries, he believes strongly in the mantra "the journey is the reward".

His other interests include: camping, coffee, dogs, gaming, reading, singing, table tennis, and vintage technology.
