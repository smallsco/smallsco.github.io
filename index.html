Scott Small

Scott is a senior engineer at Flytographer, a travel photography broker based out of Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. He has over fifteen years experience as a web developer, having worked in the higher education, performance marketing, and data analytics industries.

When he's not hacking away at work, or on a side project, he fuels a passion for travel. Having experienced four continents, and over twenty-five countries (as of 2018), he believes strongly in the mantra "the journey is the reward".

His other interests include: camping, coffee, dogs, gaming, hiking, reading, and singing.
